Chicken Burgers

Simple but healthy (somewhat) and full of protein

I would like to give an inspirational description of this recipe but alas, it's just a chicken burger.

Hungover? Lazy? Just a craving? Yes, give in and return to the chicken burger.

Please note that you can exchange, add or leave out any ingredients to this recipe, follow your personal tastes!

Ingredients

  • One pound extra-lean ground chicken
  • Half a cup of Italian-seasoned bread crumbs, divided
  • Half a small onion, finely grated
  • One egg
  • Two gloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix ground chicken, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, onion, egg, and garlic in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Moisten hands and shape chicken mixture, 2 tablespoons at a time, into flat, oval-shaped patties.
  3. Spread remaining 1/4 cup bread crumbs in a shallow dish. Dip patties in bread crumbs to coat.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook patties in hot oil until deeply browned on the bottoms, 5 to 6 minutes. Turn patties and cook until other sides are browned, 3 to 4 minutes more.
